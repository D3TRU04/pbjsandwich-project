how to make a pb&j sandwich?
step 1: gather ingredients & tools

- two slices of bread (white, wheat, or your preference)

- peanut butter

- jelly (any flavor you like)

- a butter knife or a spoon

- a clean plate

step 2: assemble the sandwich

- place the two slices of bread on the clean plate.

- using a butter knife or a spoon, spread a generous amount of peanut butter on one slice of bread.

- next, spread an equally generous amount of jelly on the other slice of bread.

step 3: put it together

- take the slice of bread with peanut butter and place it on top of the slice with jelly, creating a sandwich.

- press the two slices of bread gently together to make sure they stick.

- if you prefer, you can cut the sandwich diagonally or straight down the middle to create two halves.
